About the Symposium

The Two-day Symposium on Advances in Thermo-Fluid Sciences is a premier event that brings together researchers, practitioners, and enthusiasts in the fields of thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and heat transfer. The symposium will be held at the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati from December 9-10, 2025.
