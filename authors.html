Information for Authors

The Two-day Symposium on Advances in Thermo-Fluid Sciences invites researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to contribute to the field through poster presentations. We encourage all participants to showcase their work through this interactive format.

Poster Presentations

Poster Presentations

All research work will be presented through posters. This format provides an excellent opportunity for detailed discussions with symposium participants and allows for visual representation of your research findings.

Poster Presentation Guidelines

Poster Size

Posters should be A0 size (841mm Ã— 1189mm) in portrait orientation.

Typography

Use clear, readable fonts with a minimum size of 24pt for text and 36pt for headings.

Design

Include a clear title, author information, abstract, methodology, results, and conclusions. Use graphs and figures to illustrate key points.

Presentation Time

Be prepared to present your poster during the designated poster session (approximately 5 minutes).

Submission Process

1

Abstract Submission

Submit an abstract (300-500 words) describing your research work through the symposium website.

2

Review Process

All submissions will undergo a peer review process by experts in the field.

3

Acceptance Notification

Authors will be notified of acceptance and the recommended presentation format.

4

Registration

At least one author must register for the symposium to present the work.
