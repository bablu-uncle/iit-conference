About the Symposium

Join us for a transformative two-day symposium exploring the latest advances in Thermo-Fluid Sciences at IIT Guwahati.

The Symposium on Advances in Thermo-Fluid Sciences brings together leading researchers, industry experts, and academics to explore groundbreaking developments in fluid mechanics, heat transfer, and thermal sciences. This prestigious event offers a unique platform for knowledge exchange and collaboration.

Through keynote speeches, technical sessions, and interactive workshops, participants will gain insights into cutting-edge research and emerging trends that are shaping the future of thermo-fluid sciences.
